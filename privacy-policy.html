Privacy Policy for QuranBro

Last updated: 24 July 2024

Introduction

Welcome to QuranBro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, spiritual experience with our app. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our Quran reading and listening application.

Information We Collect

While QuranBro is designed to minimize data collection, we do use Crashlytics, a crash reporting service, to improve app stability:

  • Crash Reports: When the app crashes, Crashlytics collects technical information about the crash, including device state, operating system, and details about the crash itself. This does not include any personal information or content related to your Quran reading or listening.

Information We Do Not Collect

Apart from crash reports, QuranBro does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not require user accounts, and we do not track individual user activities related to Quran reading or listening.

Permissions We Use

Our app requires certain permissions to function properly:

  1. Internet Access: We use internet access to provide Quran content, including text and audio, and to send crash reports.
  2. Network State: This permission allows us to check if you're connected to the internet to provide seamless access to Quran content.
  3. Notifications: We may send notifications related to app updates or daily reminders if you choose to enable them.
  4. Foreground Service: This permission is used to allow audio playback when the app is in the background.

How We Use These Permissions

  • Internet and Network State: These permissions are used to download and stream Quran content and to send crash reports when necessary.
  • Notifications: If enabled, we use this to send you app-related notifications. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • Foreground Service: This allows our app to continue playing Quran recitations even when your device's screen is off or you're using other apps.

Third-Party Services

QuranBro uses Crashlytics, a crash reporting service provided by Google Firebase, to help us improve app stability. Crashlytics collects crash data as described above. For more information about Crashlytics' data practices, please refer to the Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through Crashlytics. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access or delete crash report data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
